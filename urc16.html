    • URC 2016

    University Rover Challenge 2016 was organized on 2nd, 3rd and 4th June. This was the first time we were participating in the event. 63 teams registered for the competition and 28 were selected for the competition at Mars Station, Utah.
    We stood 13th all over the world.
    Our rover was ranked second best in India and Asia.
